## Project Overview
This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Amazon Prime Video catalog to extract meaningful insights about movies and TV shows available on the platform. The analysis focuses on content distribution, genres, release trends, ratings, runtime, countries, and key contributors (actors, directors).

The goal is to transform raw streaming data into **actionable insights** that can support content strategy, recommendation systems, and business decisions.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze the distribution of **Movies vs TV Shows**
- Study **content growth trends over the years**
- Identify **popular genres and ratings**
- Explore **country-wise content availability**
- Examine **cast and crew contributions**
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing for reliable analysis

- Age certification distribution
- Runtime analysis
- Country-wise content production

---

## Key Insights
- Movies dominate the Amazon Prime catalog compared to TV Shows
- Significant growth in content production after 2015
- Drama and Comedy are the most common genres
- Majority of content targets mature audiences
- Strong contribution from US-based productions
- Certain actors and directors appear repeatedly across titles
